The Boulevard of the Muses

Moving from shadow to shadow down the wide Boulevard of the Muses, you feel very much like the intrepid adventurer hurrying to rescue his beloved lady. You are so caught up in this delightful little daydream that you don’t notice the six Watchmen following your erratic shadow-to-shadow progression down the street till you go one shadow-to-shadow progression down the street till you go one shadow too many and find yourself caught in the glare of their lanterns.

Who do you think you are—the unnatural offspring of the Three Musketeers and Michael York?
